## June 16 2026

### Work Completed
-Found a clean, synthesized dataset from Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sayantanroy10121999/egyptian-hieroglyphs-dataset, covering 1072 different classes, with 100 images per class.
-Images are mapped using the Unicode Egyptian Hieroglyph block (U+13000 to U+1342F) and are grayscale images.
-Low variation among the images, as only the thickness of the pixels are randomized.
